# PART 1: CORE REVENUE MODEL

## Primary: Paid Property Reports

User enters a rental property address. Gets a free summary, pays for the full report.

### Pricing Tiers

| Product | Price | What's Included | Data Cost |
|---------|-------|----------------|-----------|
| **Free Check** | £0 | EPC rating, council tax band, area crime snapshot, flood risk level, broadband estimate | Free APIs |
| **Rental Safety Report** | £3.99 | Everything free + ownership verification, HMO licence status, damp/mould risk, detailed crime, schools, transport, noise, full area score | £3 Land Registry title |
| **Full Renter's Report** | £6.99 | Safety Report + rent fair price comparison, total monthly cost calculator (rent + bills + council tax + commute), rights checklist, nearby listings comparison | Same + data processing |
| **Moving Bundle** | £9.99 | Full Report for 2 addresses + side-by-side comparison + area comparison + "which is better for you?" recommendation | £6 (2x Land Registry) |

### Why These Prices Work

- Tenants are about to commit £1,200 deposit + £15,600/year rent = massive stakes
- No competitor exists at any price point for this specific product
- Conveyancing searches cost £250-450 (for buyers); this is the renter equivalent at 2% of the cost
- International tenants (highest scam risk) are most willing to pay
- A scam loss averages £1,000-3,000; £3.99-6.99 is insurance

### Conversion Funnel

```
Monthly visitors:        50,000
Enter address:           20,000 (40%)
See free results:        18,000 (90% completion)
Shown premium upsell:    18,000
Click "Get full report": 3,600 (20%)
Complete payment:        1,800 (50% checkout)
= 3.6% overall conversion
```

### Revenue Projections

| Traffic | Conversion | Reports/Month | Avg Price | Monthly Revenue | Annual Revenue |
|---------|-----------|---------------|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| 10,000/mo | 3.0% | 300 | £5.50 | £1,650 | £19,800 |
| 25,000/mo | 3.5% | 875 | £5.50 | £4,813 | £57,750 |
| 50,000/mo | 3.5% | 1,750 | £5.50 | £9,625 | £115,500 |
| 100,000/mo | 4.0% | 4,000 | £5.50 | £22,000 | £264,000 |
| 250,000/mo | 4.0% | 10,000 | £5.50 | £55,000 | £660,000 |

Data cost per report: ~£3 (Land Registry title) + £0 (free APIs)
**Gross margin: 45-55% on £3.99 tier, 65-75% on £6.99+ tiers**
