# REGULATORY LANDSCAPE

## Renters' Rights Act 2025

This is the game-changer. Key provisions:

| Provision | Impact | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **Abolition of Section 21 (no-fault evictions)** | Tenants can stay indefinitely if they pay rent and don't breach tenancy | In force 2025 |
| **Mandatory PRS Database (Landlord Register)** | All landlords must register; public data on every rental property | **Late 2026** |
| **Decent Homes Standard for rentals** | Minimum property standards with enforcement | Phasing in 2025-2027 |
| **Awaab's Law extension** | Strict timelines for damp/mould repairs (emergency: 24hr, hazard: 7 days) | 2025 |
| **Pet rights** | Landlords cannot unreasonably refuse pets | 2025 |
| **Rent increases limited** | Once per year, market rate only, tribunal can challenge | 2025 |
| **New Ombudsman** | Mandatory landlord membership, binding dispute resolution | 2025-2026 |

**The PRS Database is the critical data source.** It will contain:
- Landlord name and contact
- All properties they own/manage
- Compliance status (gas safety, EPC, licensing)
- Enforcement actions
- Potentially tenant complaints/ratings

**Build RenterCheck now; the PRS Database launches your premium features in late 2026.**

## Current Legal Requirements for Landlords

| Requirement | Law | Penalty for Non-Compliance |
|-------------|-----|---------------------------|
| Gas safety check (annual) | Gas Safety Regulations 1998 | £6,000 fine + up to 6 months prison |
| EPC rating minimum E | Energy Efficiency Regulations 2015 | £5,000 fine |
| Deposit protection (within 30 days) | Housing Act 2004 | 1-3x deposit compensation to tenant |
| Right to Rent check | Immigration Act 2014 | £3,000 per tenant fine |
| HMO licence (3+ tenants, 2+ households) | Housing Act 2004 | Unlimited fine + rent repayment order (up to 12 months' rent) |
| Electrical safety (every 5 years) | Electrical Safety Standards 2020 | £30,000 fine |
| Smoke/CO alarms | Smoke & CO Alarm Regulations 2015 | £5,000 fine |
| How to Rent guide provided | Deregulation Act 2015 | Cannot serve Section 21 notice |

# COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

## Direct Competitors (Almost None)

| Competitor | What | Price | Traffic | Weakness |
|-----------|------|-------|---------|----------|
| **None** | No UK service specifically checks a rental property for tenants | - | - | The market doesn't exist yet |

## Adjacent Services

| Service | What | Price | Limitation |
|---------|------|-------|-----------|
| OpenRent | Landlord platform, some property verification | Free for tenants | Landlord-facing, not a check tool |
| SpareRoom | Room listings, basic info | Free | Room-focused, no property intelligence |
| Shelter housing advice | Free legal guidance | Free | Advice only, no data/checks |
| Citizens Advice | Rights information | Free | Generic, not property-specific |
| RateYourLandlord.org | Landlord review site | Free | Tiny, student-focused, very few reviews |
| MarksOutOfTenancy.com | Landlord reviews | Free | Same, tiny |
| HMO Check (councils) | HMO licence register | Free | Each council has own system, fragmented |
| EPC register | Energy certificate lookup | Free | Just energy data, no context |
| Land Registry | Property ownership | £3/title | Manual, not consumer-friendly |

## What Does NOT Exist

1. **Single "enter address, check everything" tool for tenants** - Nobody combines EPC + ownership + licensing + safety + area data for a specific rental
2. **Landlord track record checker** - No way to check if a landlord has enforcement actions, complaints, or a history of disrepair
3. **Deposit protection verification** - No single tool checks all three schemes (DPS, MyDeposits, TDS) at once
4. **"Is this rental a scam?" checker** - Cross-reference listing with Land Registry ownership
5. **Damp/mould risk assessment** - Based on EPC data, property age, local moisture levels
6. **Rental price comparison** - "Is this rent fair for this area?" with data backing
7. **Rights checker** - "Based on your property, here are your specific legal rights"
8. **Cost calculator** - Total monthly cost (rent + bills estimate + council tax + commute)
