# RenterCheck - Feature Ideas
## Compiled April 2026

---

# 1. RENTAL SCAM DETECTOR

User enters a listing URL (Rightmove, Zoopla, OpenRent, SpareRoom, Facebook Marketplace). AI:
- Extracts the listed address
- Cross-references with Land Registry ownership
- Checks if the "landlord" name matches the registered owner
- Flags if the property is listed at multiple different prices
- Flags if photos appear on other listings (reverse image search)
- Checks if the deposit scheme referenced is real

**Red/amber/green traffic light system.**

**Why it matters:** Rental fraud costs £13.5M/year (reported; actual likely 3-5x higher). International tenants and students are most vulnerable. No tool currently does this.

---

# 2. TOTAL MONTHLY COST CALCULATOR

Goes beyond rent to show TRUE monthly cost:
- Rent
- Council tax (by band, with single person discount if applicable)
- Energy bills (estimated from EPC rating + property size)
- Water rates (by water company + property)
- Broadband (cheapest deal for the address)
- TV Licence (if applicable)
- Contents insurance estimate
- Commute cost (from address to workplace)
- **Total monthly outgoing**

Compare two properties: "Property A costs £1,100/mo rent but £1,650 total. Property B costs £1,200/mo rent but £1,580 total."

**Why it matters:** Tenants choose on rent alone but the total cost can differ by £200+/month between similar-rent properties due to energy efficiency, council tax band, and commute.

---

# 3. DAMP & MOULD RISK ASSESSMENT

Based on:
- EPC data (insulation, heating type, ventilation)
- Property age and construction type
- Local humidity and rainfall data
- Property orientation (north-facing = higher risk)
- Known damp issues from environmental health data (if available)

Output: Risk score + specific risks identified + your rights under Awaab's Law + template letter to landlord.

**Why it matters:** Damp and mould is the #1 condition complaint. Awaab's Law gives tenants powerful new rights but most don't know about them or how to use them.

---

# 4. LANDLORD REPORT CARD (Post PRS Database)

When the PRS Database launches (late 2026):
- Landlord name and registration status
- Number of properties they manage
- Compliance history (gas safety, EPC, licensing)
- Enforcement actions against them
- Response time to repairs (if tracked)
- Tenant satisfaction (if rated)
- How long they've been a landlord

**Why it matters:** This is the killer feature that turns RenterCheck from useful to essential. First mover advantage on the PRS Database = market ownership.

---

# 5. DEPOSIT PROTECTION VERIFIER

Single check across all three UK deposit protection schemes:
- Deposit Protection Service (DPS)
- MyDeposits
- Tenancy Deposit Scheme (TDS)

Enter your name + address + approximate deposit amount. Check if it's protected.

If NOT protected: explain that the tenant can claim 1-3x the deposit amount as compensation + template letter + tribunal guidance.

**Why it matters:** ~10% of deposits may not be properly protected. The penalty is severe (1-3x deposit). Most tenants have no idea they can check or claim.

---

# 6. RIGHTS CHECKER (PERSONALISED)

Interactive questionnaire:
- Are you in England, Wales, Scotland, or NI? (different laws)
- How long have you lived there?
- What type of tenancy? (AST, rolling, lodger, etc.)
- Do you have a written tenancy agreement?
- How many tenants share the property?
- When did you last have a gas safety check?
- Has your landlord given you the "How to Rent" guide?

Output: Personalised rights summary with specific actionable items.
"Your landlord cannot evict you with Section 21 because they haven't provided the How to Rent guide."

**Why it matters:** Tenant rights are complex and vary by situation. A personalised assessment is far more valuable than generic guidance.

---

# 7. REPAIR REQUEST GENERATOR

When a tenant has a repair issue:
- Select issue type (damp, heating, plumbing, electrical, structural, pests)
- Answer severity questions
- AI generates:
  1. **Polite initial request letter** to landlord
  2. **Formal notice** citing specific legislation and timelines
  3. **Council complaint template** if landlord doesn't respond
  4. **Evidence log template** (photos, dates, communications)

Escalation ladder: informal > formal > council > tribunal.

**Why it matters:** Most tenants either suffer in silence or escalate too aggressively. A structured, legally-grounded escalation path helps both sides.

---

# 8. RENT FAIR PRICE TOOL

"Is this rent fair for this area?"
- Enter address + listed rent
- Compare against ONS median rent for the area + property type
- Show range (25th to 75th percentile)
- Factor in property size, EPC rating, furnished/unfurnished
- Show recent rental trends (going up or down?)
- Show if rent is fair/below average/above average

**Why it matters:** Tenants have no data to negotiate. "Your rent is 15% above the median for a 2-bed in this postcode" is powerful information.

---

# 9. MOVING CHECKLIST & TIMELINE

Interactive checklist for moving into a rental:
- [ ] Check deposit is protected (within 30 days)
- [ ] Receive prescribed information
- [ ] Receive How to Rent guide
- [ ] Receive gas safety certificate
- [ ] Receive EPC certificate
- [ ] Check smoke/CO alarms work
- [ ] Take dated photos of everything (inventory evidence)
- [ ] Note meter readings
- [ ] Set up utilities
- [ ] Register council tax
- [ ] Update address (DVLA, bank, GP, electoral roll)

Timeline with reminders. Each item links to relevant rights info.

**Why it matters:** Creates ongoing engagement after the initial check. Natural home for affiliate links (utility switching, insurance, broadband).

---

# 10. AREA COMPARISON FOR RENTERS

Different from PostcodeCheck's general area comparison. Specifically for renters:
- Average rent by property type
- Deposit amounts
- Rental supply (how many listings currently)
- Time properties stay on market
- Landlord quality indicators (when PRS data available)
- Transport links to work
- Budget breakdown at median rent

"Can I afford to rent in [Area A] vs [Area B]?"

**Why it matters:** Renters often choose between 2-3 areas. A renter-specific comparison that includes rent levels, availability, and total cost is unique.

---

# 11. LISTING MONITOR

Set up alerts:
- "Tell me when a 2-bed under £1,200 appears in [postcode]"
- Auto-run RenterCheck on new listings
- Flag listings with red flags (below market rent, new landlord, no EPC)
- Priority notification for "green flag" listings

**Why it matters:** Subscription/recurring revenue. Keeps users engaged. Natural extension of the check product.
